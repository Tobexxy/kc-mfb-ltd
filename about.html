About Us

Welcome To Our Company

KC Microfinance Bank is an innovative retail finance bank institution licensed by CBN to offer permissible financial products and services that suit the lifestyle of our diverse clientele. The Bank is poised to building an inclusive and sustainable institution that can last forever with the ultimate goal of servicing of our clients’ financial aspiration with utmost care, hence, building an everlasting relationship. With over ₦200M share capital base, the Bank was incorporated to engage in all kinds of permissible financial services and products amongst which are but not limited to:
• Loans and Advances
• Savings
• Investment
• Financial and Advisory Services
 KC Microfinance Bank Limited provides loans to Individuals, Small Business Operators (SMEs) as well as highly organized private limited liability companies and organizations via electronic channels. Our products are designed to meet the needs and financial aspirations of our target customers which Banks and other special purpose development Banks cannot readily provide. We offer competitive rates on our savings and fixed deposit accounts, as we are duly regulated by CBN and Insured by NDIC. The Bank is managed by seasoned professionals with high integrity and a Board of Directors comprising of individuals with impeccable and proven track records in their various fields of endeavors thereby bringing several years of experience to bear upon the Board.
